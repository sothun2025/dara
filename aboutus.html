About KimhutCafé

KimhutCafé is more than just a coffee shop – it’s a place where friends meet, where students find inspiration, and where every customer is part of our family.

Founded in 2024 in Phnom Penh, our mission is to bring the freshest flavors and warmest service to our community. We blend traditional Cambodian hospitality with a modern café experience, offering a menu full of delicious drinks and snacks made from the finest ingredients.

Our Values

  • Freshness & Quality Ingredients
  • Exceptional Customer Service
  • Community & Friendship
  • Innovation in Taste

Our Story

Our founders started KimhutCafé with a passion for coffee and a vision for a welcoming space where everyone feels at home. Today, we serve hundreds of happy customers each week – from students to business professionals, families to travelers.

Whether you’re looking for a quick coffee, a refreshing smoothie, or a cozy spot to relax, KimhutCafé is always here for you.
